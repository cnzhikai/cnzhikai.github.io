 
## About

I am a Ph.D. student at Integrated Devices, Electronics, and Systems (IDEAS) group in the Department of Information Technology and Electrical Engineering (D-ITET), ETH Zurich. My (tentative) doctoral theis is entitled *Mechanical Micro-Devices Co-Integrated with CMOS for Biomedical, Robotic and Optical Applications*. In my research, I develop platforms that integrate CMOS electronics with innovative devices fabricated through CMOS-compatible processes. Additionally, I am passionate about leveraging machine learning to enhance the functionality and broaden the impact of these platforms.

I hold an M.Sc. in Biomedical Engineering from ETH Zurich and a B.Eng. in Microelectronics from Xi'an Jiaotong University. Additionally, I gained research experience as a visiting intern at McGill University. Feel free to contact me at zhhuang@ethz.ch for any inquiries.
