About

I am a Ph.D. student in Information Technology and Electrical Engineering at ETH Zürich, working in the Integrated Devices, Electronics, and Systems (IDEAS) Group under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Hua Wang. Prior to my PhD, I obtained my M.Sc. in Biomedical Engineering at ETH Zurich, a B.Eng. in Microelectronics from Xi’an Jiaotong University, and was a visiting research intern at the Department of Physics at McGill University. Feel free to reach out for any questions or collaboration opportunities.

Research

I am mainly interested in the following three areas:
  • Bio-CMOS Interfaces ISSCC (2026)BioCAS (2024)
    Development of scalable sensing and actuation interfaces on CMOS platforms for applications in life sciences and biotechnology, with a particular focus on 3D bio-CMOS interfaces.
  • Biomedical Integrated Circuits VLSI (2024)ESSERC (2024)
    Development of CMOS integrated circuits for cellular/molecular sensing and actuation.
  • Machine Learning at Edge DATE (2022)
    Improving data and energy efficiency through analog in-sensor computing and/or digital hardware acceleration.
