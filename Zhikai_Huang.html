Single-cell Capture Microtools

  • Untethered single-cell microgrippers for active biopsy Nano Letters (2020)
    Magnetically guided, thermally actuated microgrippers capable of excising and capturing individual cells from confined environments.
  • Soft hydrogel microgripper using microactuators Visit W3Schools
    Thermoresponsive PNIPAAm-based grippers enabling gentle capture and release of fragile living cells.
  • Self-assembled microcages for selective cell capture Optics Express (2021)
    Self-assembled microcage structures that confine individual cells without continuous external force.

Organoids and Tissue Engineering Manipulation

  • Microfluidic trapping of intestinal organoids Micromachines (2022)
    Geometric trapping with perfusion bypass to immobilize organoids while avoiding pressure-induced deformation.
  • 3D-printed compliant micro-tweezers Micromachines (2021)
    High-resolution micro-tweezers for pick-and-place manipulation of soft organoids and spheroids.
  • Magnetically actuated microcages Frontiers in Bioengineering & Biotechnology (2023)
    Magnetic nanoparticle-doped microcages enabling remote cell entrapment and 3D organization.

Minimally Invasive Biopsy and Clinical Translation

  • Untethered microgrippers for upper urinary tract biopsy Advanced Healthcare Materials (2024)
    Catheter-deliverable microgrippers actuated near body temperature and retrieved magnetically for minimally invasive tissue sampling.

Emerging Trend

Integration of microfabrication, soft materials, and remote actuation (magnetic, thermal, optical) with robotic micromanipulation is enabling standardized assembly and positioning of organoids and tissues for reproducible drug screening and disease modeling.
